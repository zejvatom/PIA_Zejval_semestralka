m [kg] - mass of the car
vmax [m/s] - max speed
mi [1] - tyre friction coefficient
h [mm] - height of the COG
L [mm] - wheelbase
T [mm] - track of the car
xCOG [1] - x COG position from front axle
yCOG [1] - y COG position from right side
P [kW] - power of the motor
WD [mm] - wheel diameter
dr [1] - final drive ratio
rpm [1] - rpm of the motor for max torque
t_shift [s] - time to shift gear
gear [1] - number of gears
